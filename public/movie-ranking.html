The Best Movies According to Jay

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Avengers: Endgame

Directed by Anthony and Joe Russo, "Avengers: Endgame" is part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) and serves as the culmination of a 22-film story arc.
The film features an ensemble cast of superheroes as they unite to defeat the villain Thanos and save the universe. "Avengers: Endgame" broke numerous box office records,
becoming the highest-grossing film of all time with a worldwide box office gross of over $2.79 billion.

Jurassic Park

Directed by Steven Spielberg, "Jurassic Park" is a science fiction action film based on the novel by Michael Crichton. The film follows a group of scientists
and visitors who become trapped in a theme park filled with genetically recreated dinosaurs. "Jurassic Park" revolutionized the use of computer-generated imagery
(CGI) and set a new standard for visual effects in the industry. It became a massive success, grossing over $1 billion worldwide and spawning a highly successful franchise.

The Dark Knight

Directed by Christopher Nolan, "The Dark Knight" is the second installment in Nolan's Batman trilogy. The film features Christian Bale as Batman and Heath Ledger in an
iconic performance as the Joker. With its gritty tone and intense action sequences, "The Dark Knight" was praised by critics and audiences alike. It grossed over $1 billion
worldwide and became a cultural phenomenon, influencing the superhero genre and setting a new standard for comic book adaptations.
